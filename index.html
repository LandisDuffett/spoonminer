PLAYER

MOON MINER

  1. Mining: To start mining, click anywhere on the moon. To begin with, you will yield one portion of cheese for every click.
  2. Upgrading: Once you have enough cheese, you can use the cheese to purchase equipment. Each piece of equipment will increase your yield by a specified amount:
    • Pick axes increase your yield by 1 portion for each pick axe on each click.
    • Carts increase your yield by 5 portions for each cart on each click.
    • Rovers and mousebots are automated machines and so will increase your yield automatically every 3 seconds:
      • rovers increase your yield by 20 every 3 seconds
      • mousebots increase your yield by 100 every 3 seconds
    Note that the price of each type of equipment increases after each purchase.
  3. Time: To begin with, you have 3 minutes to reach your first goal: mine 1000 portions of cheese. If you reach your goal before time runs out, you are awarded an extra 3 minutes. This process is repeated for the rest of the game. Keep in mind that while the time bonus is always 3 minutes, your goal will increase by a factor of 3 each time a time bonus is awarded. If your time runs out before you reach the next goal, the game is over.
